F. Nezuko in the Clearing
time limit per test
1 second
memory limit per test
256 megabytes
input
standard input
output
standard output

Nezuko suddenly woke up on the number line at point 00 and has hh health points. She wants to reach point dd. In one turn, she can do exactly one of two things:

  • rest in the shade and increase her current health by 11;
  • move from her current position xx to x+1x+1.

Each movement decreases Nezuko's health; if the movement is the jj-th consecutive movement, her health will decrease by jj points. If as a result of a move Nezuko's health drops to 00 or below, she cannot make that move.

For example, if Nezuko initially had 77 health points and d=4d=4, her moves could look like this:

  1. Move from 00 to 11 and decrease health by 11. Now she is at point 11 with 66 health points.
  2. Move from 11 to 22 and decrease health by 22. Now she is at point 22 with 44 health points.
  3. Move from 22 to 33 and decrease health by 33. Now she is at point 33 with 11 health point.
  4. Rest and restore 11 health point. Now she is at point 33 with 22 health points.
  5. Move from 33 to 44 and decrease health by 11. Now she is at point 44 with 11 health point.

Find the minimum number of turns required for her to reach point dd.

Input

Each test consists of several test cases.

The first line contains one integer tt (1≤t≤1041≤t≤104) — the number of test cases. The following describes the test cases.

The first line of each test case contains two integers hh and dd (1≤h,d≤109)(1≤h,d≤109) — the number of health points and the destination point, respectively.

Output

For each test case, output one number — the minimum number of turns required for Nezuko to reach point dd.

Example

3 2
1 1
5 3
2 4

Output

2

Note

In the first test case, h=3h=3, d=2d=2 the actions could be as follows:

  1. Move from 00 to 11 and decrease health by 11. Now she is at point 11 with 22 health points.
  2. Rest and restore 11 health point. Now she is at point 11 with 33 health points.
  3. Move from 11 to 22 and decrease health by 11. Now she is at point 22 with 22 health points.

In total, 33 turns.

In the fourth test case, h=2h=2, d=4d=4 the actions could be as follows:

  1. Move from 00 to 11 and decrease health by 11. Now she is at point 11 with 11 health point.
  2. Rest and restore 11 health point. Now she is at point 11 with 22 health points.
  3. Move from 11 to 22 and decrease health by 11. Now she is at point 22 with 11 health point.
  4. Rest and restore 11 health point. Now she is at point 22 with 22 health points.
  5. Move from 22 to 33 and decrease health by 11. Now she is at point 33 with 11 health point.
  6. Rest and restore 11 health point. Now she is at point 33 with 22 health points.
  7. Move from 33 to 44 and decrease health by 11. Now she is at point 44 with 11 health point.

In total, 77 turns.
